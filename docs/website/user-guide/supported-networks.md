# Supported Social Networks

This page lists every external sharing destination the plugin supports. Your site administrator controls which ones appear in the Share menu and in what order.

## External sharing networks

### Facebook

Clicking the Facebook button opens the Facebook Share dialog in a new tab. Facebook reads the activity page's Open Graph tags to display a preview of the link.

### X (formerly Twitter)

Clicking the X button opens a pre-filled post on X (twitter.com). The activity title and link are added automatically. X has its own character limit; long titles may be trimmed by the platform.

### LinkedIn

Clicking the LinkedIn button opens the LinkedIn "Share an article" form in a new tab. The activity link is pre-filled.

### WhatsApp

Clicking the WhatsApp button opens WhatsApp with the activity link ready to send. On mobile devices this launches the WhatsApp app. On desktop it opens WhatsApp Web.

### Telegram

Clicking the Telegram button opens Telegram's share interface with the activity link and title pre-filled. On mobile this may open the Telegram app.

### Pinterest

Clicking the Pinterest button opens the Pinterest "Create Pin" page. The activity link and description are pre-filled. Pinterest works best when the activity contains an image.

### Reddit

Clicking the Reddit button opens the Reddit "Submit a Link" form. The activity title and link are pre-filled. You choose which subreddit to post to after the form opens.

### WordPress

Clicking the WordPress button opens the WordPress.com "Press This" bookmarklet form. Use this to quickly republish the activity link to a WordPress.com blog.

### Pocket

Clicking the Pocket button saves the activity link directly to your Pocket reading list. You need to be logged in to Pocket in that browser session.

### Bluesky

Clicking the Bluesky button opens Bluesky's compose window with a pre-filled message that includes the activity title and link.

### E-mail

Clicking the E-mail button opens your operating system's default email client with a pre-filled subject and message body. The subject includes your site's name and the activity title. The body includes the activity link and a short message. You add recipient addresses and send from your own email client — the plugin does not send email on your behalf.

### Copy Link

Clicking Copy Link copies the activity's permalink to your clipboard. A small "Link Copied!" tooltip confirms the action. No new tab or window opens. Paste the link anywhere you need it.

## Internal reshare destinations

These are not social networks — they are destinations inside your community. See [Resharing Internally](./resharing-internally.md) for full details.

| Destination | Requires |
|---|---|
| My Profile | Being logged in |
| A Group | Being a member of that group and the Groups component being active |
| A Friend | The Friends component being active and having confirmed friends |

## Which networks does my site show?

The Share menu on your site shows only the networks your administrator has enabled. The order also reflects the administrator's choice. If you want a network added that is not showing, contact your site administrator.
